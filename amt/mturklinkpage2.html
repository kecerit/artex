Decision-Making Experiment: Artex



Description of the Task


  • Purpose: Scientific experiment to understand how individuals interact and make decisions in groups.
  • What you will do: You will play an interactive game with other human participants. You might need to wait a short while until all participants have joined the experiment.
  • Requirements:
    1. You must have not played a HIT called "Artex" before
    2. Your browser must accept cookies and have JavaScript enabled
    3. Only the browsers Chrome >= 20, Safari >= 6, Firefox >= 20, IE >= 10, Opera >= 12 are allowed to play
    4. Due to the special game interface, you cannot participate from tablets or mobile phones
    5. Due to the special game interface, your screen must have a resolution of 1366x768px or more
  • Time required: Approximately 25 to 35 minutes.
  • Compensation: You will receive 1 dollar for completing the task, plus a variable bonus. The exact amount of the bonus depends on your choices and the choices of other participants in the game. We expect the bonus to vary between 2 and 4 dollars.


Important


  • Confidentiality: In order to guaranteee the unbiased behavior of future participants of this scientific experiment, you agree not to disclose information about the rules of the game, its interface, or the strategy of play to other turkers and/or to the general public.
  • Trouble Ticket: In case of any technical issues, or a delay with the payment, please search for a HIT called "Artex Trouble Ticket" and report your issues there.
  • Synchronicity: This is a synchronous task with other human participants that must wait for each other. If you accept this HIT don't let the others wait for you, you must begin the experiment immediately!


Consent Form to Participate in Scientific Research


We would like to invite you to participate in a web-based online experiment. The experiment is part of a research study whose purpose is to study how groups perform decisions. A single experimental session should take about 25-35 minutes to complete.

You must be at least 18 years old to participate in these experiments.

The decision to participate in this research project is voluntary. You do not have to participate and you can refuse to answer any question. Even if you begin the web-based experiments, you can stop at any time by closing your browser.

There are no foreseeable risks or discomforts to you for taking part in this study. We anticipate that it will take you as little as 25 minutes and as many as 35 minutes to complete this task. However, you will only be rewarded for completing the task, not for the length of time you participate.

There will be compensated for good faith participation in this experiment. Submitted HITs will be rejected if they are completed in a manner that appears to be random, incomplete, or otherwise negligent.

Your part in this study is anonymized to the researchers. Researchers at the Army Research Office and Office of Naval Research may have access to anonymized experimental research records. However, because of the nature of electronic systems, it is possible that respondents could be identified by some electronic record associated with the response. Neither the researcher nor anyone involved with this study will be capturing those data. Any reports or publications based on this research will use only group data and will not identify you or any individual as being affiliated with this project. The only individually-identifying data we receive from Mechanical Turk are your unique identifier and your country. Your data are also stored on Mechanical Turk's servers, and the data there are subject to the Amazon Mechanical Turk Privacy Notice and Participation Agreement.
