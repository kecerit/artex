The experiment is divided in two parts: Preparation and Game.

Part 1: Preparation

In Part 1 you will not interact with other players, and you cannot earn points unless explicitly mentioned on the screen. Part 1 involves three steps:
  • First, some basic psychological and demographics characteristics will be collected.
  • Secondly, the instructions of the game played in Part 2 will be shown to you, followed by a short understanding quiz.
  • Finally, you will play in practice mode to get familiar with the game interface.

Part 2: Game

In this part you will interact with other human participants. You might need to wait a short while until enough participants are connected.
  • The game will be repeated for a certain number of rounds, and in each round you will have a chance to earn points based on your actions, and the actions of other players.
  • After the game, you will be shown a questionnaire consisting in a short compulsory part, followed by a series of optional questions.

Payout scheme

If you complete the whole experiment (Part 1 + Part 2) you will receive:
  • a fixed reward of 1 USD, and
  • a variable bonus depending on the total number of points collected during the experiment. Every X points you will earn 1 USD.
