Welcome to the Art Exhibition Game!

You just became part of a community of 9 painters (including you). The goal of the community is to collectively decide which paintings can be exposed in 3 (three) artistic exhibitions.

The game is divided in rounds, and in each of these you will act both as an artist and as an art critic.

Each round is divided in three steps, as follows:

  • Step 1: Each player produces 1 (one) painting, and submits it to one of three exhibitions (named A, B, or C). All the three exhibitions are equally prestigious to the players.
  • Step 2: Each player reviews 3 (three) of the 8 (eight) paintings produced by the other artists. The review is done by assigning a rating on a scale from 1 to 9 to the painting. Self review is not allowed.
  • Step 3: All the paintings that received an average review score greater than 7 (seven) are put on display in the exhibition to which they were submitted.

For each paining that is put on display in one of the exhibitions you will receive 0.XX CHF.

The game will ends after 50 (fifty) rounds, or after 1 hour.

You wil now play a test round during which you will be guided through the three steps of the game and you learn to use the graphical interface to produce your painintgs.
